Get ready to find out ... A little bit about me

My name is Christian Cluff. I was born in Whittier, California a little suburb outside of Los Angeles. I come from a family of 9, 3 brothers and 3 sisters, who are all my best friends. I enjoy spending time with my family and always want to have a good time with them.


I served my mission in Charolete, North Carolina speaking the spanish language. I served from 2021 to 2023 and loved every second of it. I served in 6 areas and had over 15 companions that have become life long friends.


I have had a lot of different groups of friends throughout my life but most recently these friends have brought me so much joy and friendship. I enjoy my time with them as we have spontaneous adventures and are always ready to have a party by ourselves or with others.


My favorite past time is to play sports! My favorite sports to play are Basketball, Volleyball, and Pickleball. I enjoy playing with my friends and family as well as competitivly. I have found that these sports help me to stay fit and active in my everyday life.
